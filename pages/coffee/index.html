How to make a perfect cup of coffee

Making a great cup of coffee doesn’t have to be complicated. With just a few simple steps, you can brew delicious coffee at home that rivals your favorite café.

Things you'll need

  • Freshly ground coffee beans
  • Coffee maker or French press
  • Filtered water
  • Measuring spoon or scale
  • Mug
  • Optional: milk, sugar, or flavored syrup

Steps

  1. Choose High-Quality Beans

    Start with fresh, whole coffee beans. Look for beans that were roasted within the past two weeks.

    Tip: Lighter roasts bring out floral and fruity notes, while darker roasts give a stronger, more bitter flavor.

  2. Measure your Coffee

    Use about 1 to 2 tablespoons of coffee per 6 ounces of water, depending on how strong you like it. Consistent measurement ensures consistent flavor.

  3. Grind the Beans

    Grind your beans right before brewing to preserve aroma and flavor.

    • Coarse grind for French press
    • Medium grind for drip machines
    • Fine grind for espresso

  4. Heat Your Water

    Heat filtered water to around 200°F (93°C) — just below boiling. Water that’s too hot can scorch the coffee grounds.

  5. Brew Your Coffee

    Pour hot water evenly over the grounds and let it brew.

    • French press: steep for 4 minutes
    • Drip machine: follow manufacturer instructions

  6. Serve and Enjoy

    Pour your coffee into a mug. Add milk, sugar, or flavorings if you like — or enjoy it black to taste the pure coffee flavor.

Tips

  • Store beans in an airtight container away from light and moisture.
  • Avoid keeping coffee in the fridge; it absorbs odors easily.
  • Clean your coffee equipment regularly to prevent buildup and bitterness.
